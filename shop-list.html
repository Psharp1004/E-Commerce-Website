It is a good product for office use. I received it day before yesterday and till now I am very satisfied with it. There is however a need for a pre-research before you start using this product. I recommend that you buy a 64 gb samsung EVO plus high speed card or any other brand of high speed (80 mb/s read speed) micro sd card. Before installing any other new app in the new laptop wait for the windows 10 build 10240 which comes along with the device free, to upgrade automatically to windows 10 version 1511 (i.e. November update of Windows 10 as is popularly known). It may take one day or so before new device automatically detects the need for this update.

Reason is simple with this update you can change the default location of new apps to load automatically to 64 gb micro sd card which is now d: drive. With the windows 10 updated to 1511, to change the default location go to 'settings', click 'system' then click 'storage'. Then you will see options for default location of new apps on right of the screen. With 1511 update only you can change this default to drive d i.e sd card. Idea is to save space on c: drive which is only 32 gb and not enough to take load of new apps.

Also do one more thing, in 'storage' screen only go to 'this pc' i.e. c: drive and go to 'temporary files' and remove those files to get more space. In the same screen go to 'remove old windows version' and you will save another 6 g b space. But do this only after your device has already updated windows 10 to new win 10 version i.e. 1511. don't do any other efforts to upgrade to 1511. Let it upgrade automatically. have patience. Otherwise any manual upgrade will only spoil the software already installed unless you are expert. so let it do it automatically.

Only you can keep checking in the 'update' section of the 'system' window in 'settings' to see if the new updates have been detected by the system till windows is upgraded to 1511. by doing this now you have a total of close to 100 gb space with only around 13 GB used by windows updated version on c: drive and new application will automatically get loaded to d: drive saving space in c: drive. You will have at least 15 gb space in c: drive free after all these efforts. This total of 100 gb space is not bad when you compare with macbook which has 125 gb total space and is very expensive device 55 K plus.
